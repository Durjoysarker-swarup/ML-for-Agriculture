# Fake News Detection
## ML model: Natural Language Processing

## 📋 Quick Overview

**Problem:** Classify news articles as real or fake based on text content

**Approach:** NLP (Natural Language Processing) with TF-IDF and classification

**Skills Demonstrated:**
- Text preprocessing (tokenization, stopwords, stemming)
- TF-IDF (Term Frequency-Inverse Document Frequency) vectorization
- NLP text classification pipeline
- Evaluating text-based models
- Document representation in ML

**Dataset:** Fake News Dataset (50,000+ articles, labels: Real/Fake)

---

## 🎯 Why This Project Matters

**Information Context:**
Misinformation spreads rapidly. Automated detection helps identify unreliable sources and combat disinformation.

**Agricultural Connection:**
- Detecting false agricultural advice and claims
- Identifying unreliable crop management information
- Fighting climate change misinformation in farming
- Validating agricultural news and market information
- Preventing misleading marketing of seeds/inputs
